# Intro TikTok — script

The pinned intro video for the Kinga-fronted TikTok experiment (see
`../../../channels/tiktok/experiment-brief.md`). Built from
`thoughts/assets/articles/020-my-journey-from-employee-to-running-a-business.md`,
written in personal-tone-of-voice.

**Treatment:** Kinga to camera throughout, roughly 50 seconds, no screen
recording. Tamas is named but does not appear on camera. Warm and plain, not a
pitch. Pin it to the top of the profile so it is the first thing a new visitor
taps. On-screen captions mirror the spoken words (most people watch on mute
first).

| Beat | Spoken (to camera) | On-screen text |
|---|---|---|
| Hook (0-4s) | "Only 14% of the people building apps with AI are women. I'd really like to help change that." | **Only 14% of people building with AI are women.** |
| Who (4-11s) | "Hi, I'm Kinga. I spent eight years as a product manager in tech, and at the end of 2024 I left my corporate job, because I wanted to build things that actually help people." | Kinga · ex-PM, 8 years |
| What (11-22s) | "So now it's me and my partner Tamas, a two-person company called productkind. We build Little Parrot, where we help women benefit from AI by using it to solve their everyday problems in work or life /build real products with AI, even if they've never written a line of code." | productkind · a 2-person company |
| Why (22-31s) | "Most vibe coding content out there is made by tech bros, for tech bros. I wanted something that speaks to the rest of us." | — |
| The channel (31-40s) | "So here I'll show you honestly how we run a tiny business with AI. The real prompts, and the things that actually work." | — |
| The honest bit (40-50s) | "And to be honest, a lot of our numbers aren't glamorous yet. But I'm happy to share what doesn't work too, because someone out there can learn from it. So follow along if you're building something, or you'd like to." | Free challenges & Toolkits → **LittleParrot.app** |

**Spoken search phrase** (for TikTok's search index): "building with AI" and
"Little Parrot" both land naturally in the script.

**Follow CTA** is payoff-anchored (honest behind-the-scenes of building a
company with AI, including the failures) and placed at the end, never as the
hook. No clickable link yet, so the destination is spoken and shown as a search
prompt.

Captions for TikTok and Instagram Reels are in `captions.md`.
