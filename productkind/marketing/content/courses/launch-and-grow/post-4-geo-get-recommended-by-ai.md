## Caption (exact)

A growing share of your future users won't Google "book club app". They'll ask a chat assistant like ChatGPT or Claude.ai, "what's a good way to organise my book club?", and use whatever it suggests. 💬

Getting your product into those answers has a name: GEO, Generative Engine Optimisation. Its cousin AEO covers the AI answer boxes at the top of search results. Both sound technical, and both come down to habits you can start this week: describe your product in concrete detail, add an FAQ that leads with direct answers, mark it up so machines can read it (one Lovable prompt), and get mentioned on other sites.

The comforting part: everything that helps AI tools recommend you also helps humans understand you, so none of this work is ever wasted.
