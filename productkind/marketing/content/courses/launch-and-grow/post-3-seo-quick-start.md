## Caption (exact)

Sharing your app link by hand works for the first twenty users. After that, you want Google doing some of the sharing for you. 🔍

SEO (search engine optimisation) sounds like a specialist job, and the starting version is five steps, most of them a single Lovable prompt: clean heading structure, alt text on images, a sitemap submitted to Google Search Console, and a first backlink from a product directory.

The step most people skip is the last one: patience. Appearing in search results takes weeks in the typical case, and that's normal, so set it up early and let it compound while you build.
