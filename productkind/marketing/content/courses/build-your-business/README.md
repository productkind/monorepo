# Instagram: Build Your Business (6 carousels)

Six Instagram carousels built from the **Build Your Business: From Vibe Coded
Product to Paying Customers** course (`vibe-coding-start-your-business-00`)
and its toolkits, shaped by our audience research
and the content strategy.

## Why these six (research trace)

- **This course serves the dream state directly:** money and independence
  attached to a real thing strangers use. Lovable's own survey: 8 in 10
  builders plan to monetise; 75% of women starting businesses want to be
  their own boss. First paying customer is the after-state in their words.
- **The "you don't need" post is permission content**, the audience's native
  arc ("I did not wait for permission or a co-founder or a computer science
  degree"), and the budget-conscious register ($20-30 until traction)
  respects their documented cost anxiety.
- **Community-sharing anxiety is real for this audience** (pre-apologising,
  "dumb question, but..."): post 5 gives a script for the exact moment they
  fear looking spammy.
- **Five of the six repackage existing toolkit items** (value proposition
  formula, directory list, pricing psychology cheat sheet, plus the
  outline's prepare-before-you-act arc and community template), per the
  reuse-first rule. All are saveable formulas, lists, or checklists.

## Posting notes

- Account: Little Parrot Instagram. Cadence 1 to 2 per week, so this set
  covers three to four weeks. Suggested order: post in numbered order; it
  follows the course's prepare → act → learn → earn arc, with the
  permission post (2) early because it unlocks the rest.
- CTAs name the full course title and LittleParrot.app rather than "link in
  bio": the Instagram bio holds only 5 links and we have more courses than
  slots. The UTM URL below remains for wherever the course link is actually
  placed (a bio slot, stories, comment replies):
  `https://littleparrot.app/b7455f5a-166c-4614-a4ca-be83369ff7f1/course-overview?utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=build-your-business`
  (add `&utm_content=post-1` etc. per post for per-post tracking).
- The course's first challenge (Craft Your Value Proposition) is free; CTAs
  say so.
- Carousel slides: 1080 x 1350 (4:5 portrait), same visual system as the
  other July sets.
- Sequencing note: this campaign continues Dalmie's storyline after the
  launch-and-grow set. Natural running order across campaigns:
  build-first-app → launch-and-grow → build-your-business.
