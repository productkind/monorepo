The fix is context. Tell it what the feature does, why it exists, the proof, when it's live, and who it's for.

YOUR PROMPT 
Write a short announcement to our customers about our new "Saved Dashboard Views" feature.
- What it does: save your favourite filters, reopen them in one click.
- Why: our top customer request; beta testers report real time saved.
- When: live for everyone today.
- Audience: our users, small teams and founders.
