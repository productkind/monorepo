LittleParrot.app02 / 07

The counter is your FRONTEND: everything users see and touch.

Buttons, forms, colours. It runs on their device.

Also called the client.

LittleParrot.app03 / 07

The kitchen is your BACKEND: the work customers never see.

Saving data, sending emails, taking payments.

LittleParrot.app04 / 07

The storage room is your DATABASE: where your app remembers.

Profiles, orders, posts, all organised and findable.

LittleParrot.app05 / 07

The building is a SERVER: the computer your backend and database run on.

You rent rather than buy. That's CLOUD COMPUTING, and Lovable handles it for you.

LittleParrot.app06 / 07

The phone line to the mill who supplies the flour is an API: how your app talks to other apps, like Stripe.

The API KEY is the access code. Keep it secret: it never goes in a prompt.
