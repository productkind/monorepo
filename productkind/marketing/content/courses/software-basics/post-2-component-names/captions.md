## Instagram (Collab post with Kinga)

Every part of your app has a name, and your AI builder knows them all 🧩

The gap is on our side: when we can't name the pop-up, we describe around it, and the AI guesses. So the design comes back not quite like the one in your head. Learning the words is how you get the best result.

The six components you'll use most while vibe coding are:
• buttons, with their ranks (primary, secondary, tertiary)
• cards
• modals (that's the pop-up over everything else)
• input fields and textareas
• dropdowns
• navigation bars

Once you use these names, the back-and-forth decreases: "show each note as a card with a category dropdown and a primary Save button" is one prompt and a good result.

This is the kind of vocabulary we teach women building their first app with AI. The full prompt library comes with Basics of Software for Vibe Coding on LittleParrot.app, first challenge free.

Save this for the next time you're vibe coding your app 🔖, and send it to a friend who's building one too.
