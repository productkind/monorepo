# Mission visual - personal LinkedIn post

A single, reshareable image in the spirit of the IWD visual: lead with the gap, a
rallying line, then a quiet action. Mission-forward, not product-forward. Framed
around women as founders using AI to their advantage, not learning to engineer.

Pairs with `linkedin-personal.md` as its image. (The parrot carousel goes with the
company post.)

---

## On-image copy

> In 2025, women started 44% of new businesses.
>
> Make sure your idea is one of them.
>
> With AI, you can build it yourself.
>
> Build Your First App with Lovable. Free until 23 June.

## The stats and why this framing
One fresh, hard stat (women's share of new businesses), then a reader-directed line
that makes AI meaningful rather than decorative. The stat is a good number, so "let's
change that" can't apply to it. Instead: your idea could be one of those businesses,
and AI is what lets you build it yourself. "Build it yourself" carries the
barrier-removal (you, not a developer) without spelling it out or using the tired
"no code needed" tag. Keeps it about creating your own thing, not chatting or
outsourcing, and not "becoming a developer".

**Why not a hard AI number:** searched the 2026 vibe-coding, AI-app-builder and
no-code reports. There is no clean, citable 2026 figure on women's share of people
*building* with AI. The available numbers are either general AI *usage* (chatting and
daily tool use, the consuming gap, which is also closing fast) or AI *jobs* (women
~25% of tech roles), neither of which is "women using AI to build their own thing".
The only building-specific figure is the ~1 in 5 vibe-coder stat already used on the
IWD visual. So the AI side stays aspirational rather than asserting a stale or
off-mission number.

## Sources (verify the day you post)
- Women started 44% of new businesses in 2025 (49% in 2024): Gusto, 2026 New Business
  Formation report ("How AI Is Reshaping Who Starts a Business in America").
- Context on the AI gaps that exist (usage and jobs, not building): AI Literacy
  Institute and women-in-tech 2026 syntheses.

## Design notes
- **Format:** single square image (1:1), the format that reshares best.
- **Fonts:** Space Mono for the stat and rally line, Inter for the smaller action/footer.
- **Hierarchy:** the "44%" is the hero, largest type. Consider setting it in brand
  green or the highlight gradient so it pops.
- **Style:** neobrutalist, sharp edges (0 radius), 2px solid borders, hard box shadow
  `0.25rem 0.25rem 0.125rem rgba(8 8 9 / 0.25)`. White or dark-purple background.
- **Footer:** LittleParrot.app, small, like the IWD visual. No SheBuilds link on the
  image itself (the post body carries that).
