# Campaign brief

## Campaign name
shebuilds-s03-june-2026

## What's happening, and why now
Lovable's SheBuilds season 03 is back in June 2026. It's a 48-hour buildathon: you get
free Lovable credits to build your idea, alongside hundreds of other women building
theirs. The top prize is one-to-one mentorship with Elena Verna, Head of Growth at
Lovable. SheBuilds was made so more women are the ones building with AI, which is
exactly why it's worth signalling to the people who think hackathons aren't for them.
This is the moment because applications and the free-course window are both open now.

## The offer
We made Little Parrot's Build Your First App With Lovable course free until 23 June. No application or SheBuilds
spot needed. It's the exact course many current learners first arrived through, so
it doubles as a low-pressure on-ramp for anyone curious about building with AI.

## The ask
Two asks, depending on channel:
1. Try the free course (warm/own audiences, and anyone new).
2. Pass the free-course link to a friend who's curious about building. Sharing is the priority: it's how most people find us.
Wrap up the course sharing asks into the nudge to apply for SheBuilds, especially if hackathons have felt
like they're for someone else.
