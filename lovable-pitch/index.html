What we propose

1 Ambassador content partnership

We create blended classroom materials for Lovable Ambassadors: learners study in Little Parrot, apply their skills in Lovable, and ambassadors get facilitation tools to run polished local events with zero prep overhead.

  • Structured, tested curriculum designed for non-technical audiences
  • Proven by independent adoption (Singapore)
  • Scales Lovable's reach while advancing our shared mission of closing the gender gap in AI

2 Free credits for learners

The number one complaint from our learners was running out of free Lovable credits mid-course. The full "Build Your First App" course uses roughly 15–25 credits (about 5 days of free-tier allowance).

  • A small credit allocation per learner removes the biggest drop-off point
  • Every completed course = a new Lovable user who has published a real app
  • These learners post about their apps on social media, giving Lovable free promotion
