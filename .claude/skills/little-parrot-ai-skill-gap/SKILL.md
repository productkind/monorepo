# Little Parrot – Background & Context

## What is Little Parrot
Little Parrot is a microlearning platform that offers practical, science-based courses that make building with AI and vibe coding accessible to everyone.

## Little Parrot's Mission
Our mission is to close the gender gap in AI adoption.

## Why Little Parrot Exists
AI and vibe coding make it possible for more people to build. Yet women still make up only a small share of those creating with these tools. We want to change that. Little Parrot teaches the practical skills needed to build products with AI, in a kind and supportive way.

## Target Audience
Non-technical women with business ideas who want to build.

## Ethos
We’re living through a moment that could reshape who gets to build the future. With AI app builders, anyone can turn an idea into working software using simple prompts.

Yet women are being left out. They make up a large share of online workers and business owners, but only about one in five vibe coders are women. We have seen a pattern like this before. In the early 1980s, women were entering computer science at a faster rate than men. Then personal computers were marketed mainly to boys, and that progress faded.

We don’t want to see history repeat itself.

Right now, many of the people building apps come from similar backgrounds. They absolutely belong there, but they shouldn’t be the only ones.

We care because software is a form of power, and power should be shared. When a woman builds something, she creates economic independence, solves problems that matter to her community, and becomes a role model for future generations. At Little Parrot, we’re helping women gain the skills to create value and opportunity through software.

Our microlearning approach fits into busy lives. Short, science-backed lessons make it possible to learn during coffee breaks, commutes, or after the children are asleep, without career interruptions or major sacrifices.

And this all happens in a kind, welcoming space where curiosity is encouraged, mistakes are part of learning, and everyone belongs.

## Design system
* Follow the major design principles when designing the UI: hierarchy, proximity, contrast, and balance.
* Brand colours:
    * Orange: `hsl(33 100% 68%)`
    * Green: `hsl(148 100% 47%)`
    * Gradient for highlight: `linear-gradient(120deg, #eb9600,#f1c000,#fbfb00,#aeff37,#00ed70)`
    * Gradient for background: `linear-gradient(90deg, #ffb65b20, #fdd82520, #fbfb0020, #8efd2320, #00ed7020)`
    * Background light theme color: `hsl(0 0% 100%)`
    * Foreground light theme color: `hsl(245 7% 3%)`
    * Background dark theme color: `hsl(262.9, 100%, 13.3%)` (dark purple)
    * Foreground dark theme color: `hsl(0 0% 100%)`
    * Muted font: `hsl(245 3% 48%)` (lighter gray)
* The default text font is: Google font "Inter"
* The main heading, monospace and highlight font is: Google font "Space Mono"
* Neobrutalist isometric design
    * Border radius is strictly 0 to have sharp edges
    * Borders are 2 pixels wide and not muted (border-2)
    * Strong box shadows with minimal blur and sizeable offset for an isometric style: `0.25rem 0.25rem 0.125rem rgba(8 8 9 / 0.25)`
* The design should always be edgy but welcoming and playful, using big spaces

## Business Model
Monthly subscription for 24 EUR per month. Little Parrot also offers the Open Wings Scholarship to fund lifetime subscriptions for underrepresented groups in tech.

## Little Parrot's Growth Trajectory

Little Parrot's reach has grown sharply across the SheBuilds buildathons, where its free courses are offered to participants. The clearest jump came between the December 2025 cohort (S02) and the March 2026 International Women's Day event, where every metric grew by roughly an order of magnitude.

### Results by campaign

| Metric | SheBuilds S02 (Dec 2025) | SheBuilds IWD (Mar 2026) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Site visitors | 273 | 2,603 |
| Registered | 86 | 663 |
| Started a course | 86 | 630 |
| Used free coupon code | 42 | N/A |
| Finished at least half a course | 24 | 193 |
| Finished at least one course | 16 | 132 |
| Finished both courses | 8 | 2 |
| Paid | 0 | 4 |
| Shared on LinkedIn | 0 | 18 |

The IWD figures cover roughly 23 February to 11 March 2026.

### Conversion shifts (S02 to IWD)
- Visitors to registration: 31.5% to 25.4% (lower rate, but on nearly 10x the traffic)
- Course completion from course start: 18.6% to 21%
- Shared on LinkedIn: 0% to 2.5%

### Context
- **SheBuilds S01 (Oct 2025)** was the inaugural buildathon Kinga took part in. SheBuilds reported roughly 3,000 women applying and 215 accepted. This predates Little Parrot offering its own courses to participants, so there are no Little Parrot completion figures for it.
- **SheBuilds IWD (March 2026)** was a global event where Lovable offered free credits for everyone on International Women's Day. Little Parrot offered their "Build Your First App With Lovable" course for free in the two weeks before IWD until after 2 days of the event.
- **SheBuilds S03 (June 2026)** is the next chapter. "Build Your First App With Lovable" was made free for it. Results are not in yet.

The headline story: from S02 to IWD, site traffic grew about 10x, completions of at least one course grew from 16 to 132, and the first paid conversions and organic LinkedIn shares appeared, the early signs of the share-driven acquisition loop taking hold.

### Little Parrot's Paid Users
As of June 2026, Little Parrot has 4 paid users. Overall, they had 7 paid users.

## Lovable & SheBuilds

### Why Little Parrot teaches Lovable
Lovable is the platform at the centre of Little Parrot's vibe coding courses. Little Parrot teaches Lovable specifically because Lovable actively champions women's access to vibe coding, a values alignment that makes it a natural fit for Little Parrot's mission.

### Relationship history
There is no formal partnership, but the relationship has developed organically through three SheBuilds on Lovable buildathlons:

1. **October 2025** — Kinga participated as one of 215 accepted women in the first SheBuilds cohort.
2. **December 2025** — Kinga took a support role, helping women in the second cohort via the SheBuilds Discord community.
3. **March 2026 (IWD global event)** — Kinga and Tamas offered the "Build Your First App with Lovable" course for free, enabling more women to benefit from the free Lovable credits available on International Women's Day. They also attended a SheBuilds event in Singapore organised by a local Ambassador. There, they discovered that the Lovable Ambassador had used Little Parrot course content word-for-word in her own presentation — unprompted — because it was more effective for teaching Lovable than the materials Lovable itself provides. This was taken as strong validation of the course material's quality and practical usefulness.
