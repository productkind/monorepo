# Turning Your Vibe Coded Product into a Business
This micro-course aims to give skills to start a business based on a digital product that the learner created. It goes through all the necessary steps and considerations to start a business.

## Target Audience

Non-technical women who built a digital product using an AI app builder like Lovable and want to start a business.

## Content

- How to connect GA and Posthog, explain basic ideas like session, pageview, custom events, and
  visitor info
- How to purchase a Domain, considerations when buying it
- Google workspace, and how to set up the domain with Gmail
- Explain Domain setup for sending emails
- Don't spend more money than 10-20 dollars
- Add a report a bug / feedback feature into your product
- Getting traffic to your site: Where to add your link (product hunt, betalist, https://boukar.notion.site/Ultimate-Startup-Directory-Launch-List-86-2f0af2714ad280618f46ef08b95b39a8)
- Explain what Stripe is on a high level and what is required for setting it up (e.g., Tax number). We will have a separate course about stripe and other 3rd party integrations
- Mention the "Launch and Grow Your Lovable App" course where applicable
- Use wait lists to test early and talk to your customers, start collecting email addresses you can
  reach out to when your product is ready to launch
- Explain what are lead magnets and how to use them
- find the easiest/cheapest legal framework to operate, only start a company if you have solid
  revenue or a deal requires it, setting up and operating a company is costly
- Validate your idea and product before investing too much
- Add user testimonials asap (don't fake them)
- You don’t need: A company, Investors, A perfect product
- You do need: Your customers getting value (check out "Build a Valuable Product: How to Break Down Your Idea for Vibe Coding")
- Tell what your product does to people 1on1 to learn what message works
- Consider pivoting if there is no traction
- Small but consistent amount of work week by week can get you far 
- Register on https://business.google.com/en-all/business-profile/ for improving your SEO
- Give recommendations on when to invest in your business or quit your job
- Offer simple pricing strategies — how to decide what to charge, free vs freemium vs paid, pricing psychology basics for a first product
- Suggest simple free banking solutions: Wise, Revolut
- Talk to an accountant about your tax situatuion, and the best legal framework to operate with in your location
