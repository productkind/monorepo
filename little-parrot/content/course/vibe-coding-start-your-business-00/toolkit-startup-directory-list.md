## Startup Directory List

A curated list of directories where you can submit your product to get your first visitors. All of them are free to submit to. You don't need to submit to all of them. Start with a few that fit your product and your audience.

### How to use this list

1. Start with **For early-stage and bootstrapped products** if you're just getting started
2. Reuse your assets across submissions: your value proposition, screenshots, and pain point description
3. Submit to 2-3 directories at first. See which ones bring visitors, then add more.
4. Most directories add a reference to the link when they send visitors to your site (e.g., `yourproduct.com/?ref=betalist`). You'll see this in PostHog, so you'll know which directories are actually sending you traffic.

### Tips for submitting

- **Use your value proposition** as your one-line description.
- **Add a screenshot** that shows your product in action, not a logo.
- **Write a short paragraph** about the pain point your product solves.
- **Submit to 2-3 directories** at first. See which ones bring visitors, then add more.
- **Check your backlinks** with the [Ahrefs Backlink Checker](https://ahrefs.com/backlink-checker/) (free) to see which directories are linking to your site.
