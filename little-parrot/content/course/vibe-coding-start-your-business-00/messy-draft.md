# Turning Your Vibe Coded Product into a Business
This micro-course aims to give skills to start a business based on a product that the learner created.
It goes through all the necessary steps and considerations to start a business.

## Target Audience

Non-technical women who built a product using an AI app builder like Lovable and want to start a
business.

## Content

- How to connect GA and Posthog, explain basic ideas like session, pageview, custom events, and
  visitor info
- How to purchase a Domain, considerations when buying it
- Google workspace
- Don't spend more money than 10-20 dollars
- Report a bug / feedback feature
- Where to add your link (product hunt, betalist, https://boukar.notion.site/Ultimate-Startup-Directory-Launch-List-86-2f0af2714ad280618f46ef08b95b39a8)
- Explain what is Stripe and what is required for it. We will have a course about stripe and other
  3rd party integrations
- Check out the "Launch and Grow Your Lovable App" course
- Use waiting lists to test early and talk to your customers
- What are lead magnets and how to use them
- find the easiest/cheapest legal framework to operate, only start a company if you have solid
  revenue or a deal requires it
- Validate before “officially launching”
- Add testimonials asap
- You don’t need: A company, Investors, A perfect product, You do need: Your customers getting value
  (check out "Build a Valuable Product: How to Break Down Your Idea for Vibe Coding", it also helps
  with your mission statement)
- Tell what your product does to people 1on1 to learn what message works
- Consider pivoting on no traction
- Consistent but small amount of work week by week can get you far 
- Register on https://business.google.com/en-all/business-profile/
- when to invest in your business or quit your job
- Simple pricing strategies — how to decide what to charge, free vs freemium vs paid, pricing psychology basics for a first product
- Simple free banking solutions: Wise, Revolut
- Talk to an accountant about your tax situatuion in your location
