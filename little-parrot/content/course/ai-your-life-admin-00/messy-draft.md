# AI Your Life Admin for New Moms
- Create a course that helps new moms get back time by outsourcing life admin to AI, and becoming more efficient with AI.

## Target audience
- Non-technical women
- New moms who are very busy with work and life admin

## Pain point
- They have fractured attention, foggy brain, a lot more life admin to take care of, less time
- You don't want to put a lot of work into doing a bunch of setup
- You just want to get started and get your problem solved
- Spending a lot of time to structure your workflow automation to get value out

## Needs
- Things don't fall through the cracks: How do I make sure that nothing escapes the leaky sieve of my brain that I need to do?
- I don't need to constantly think about these frees up mindspace
- Use dictation for everything
- Make progress on things that are otherwise are just weighing on me: break down large tasks and fit the pieces into my schedule
- Make life admin feel like less of a burden
- Finding the lowest friction ways to use AI assistants

## Three iPhone Shortcut Workflows for New Moms

- Use technology that's already available on an iPhone: Shortcuts, ChatGPT (no subscription needed
- The learner doesn't have to be an English speaker, maybe they will take the notes in Polish. But keep the examples and course in English, just check viability of dictating in different languages.

---

### 1. Smart Brain Dump

**What it does:** You talk for 60 seconds about everything on your mind. AI categorises each item and routes it to the right app: events → Calendar, to-dos → Reminders, everything else → Notes with different notes (e.g. shopping list, other todos).

**Trigger:** "Hey Siri, Brain Dump" or Action Button.


### 3. Baby Log

**What it does:** You say what just happened (feed, nap, nappy, mood, milestone) and AI parses it into a structured log entry, timestamped and saved to a running note. Over time, this becomes a detailed, searchable record you can use to keep track of your baby's development, and share with your paediatrician.

**Trigger:** "Hey Siri, Baby Log" or Action Button.
