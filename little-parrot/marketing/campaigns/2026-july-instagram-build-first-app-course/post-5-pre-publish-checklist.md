## Caption (exact)

Publishing your first app is a big moment, and a five-minute checklist makes it a calm one. 🚀

The steps in the carousel cover three things: the details that make your app feel finished (favicon, title, share image, a URL you can say out loud), the security check that makes it safe (errors fixed, warnings noted), and a quick walk-through on phone and desktop so you see what your first user will see.

Then the reassurance we give every learner: your published app won't appear in Google searches. Nobody finds it unless you share the link. So you can publish early, show one person, and keep improving. Waiting for perfect helps no one, least of all you.
