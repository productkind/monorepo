## Caption (exact)

There's a moment in every Lovable project where you notice you've typed "warm cream background, coral accents, friendly tone" for the fifth time. That's the moment to open Project Knowledge. 📓

It's a panel where you describe your app once (who it's for, how it looks, how it speaks, what things are called), and Lovable reads it with every prompt from then on. Your prompts get shorter, your app stays consistent, and you stop paying credits for corrections you shouldn't need.

The tip our learners love most: write down decisions, not only plans. One line like "no payment features in version 1" saves you a surprising number of unwanted suggestions.
