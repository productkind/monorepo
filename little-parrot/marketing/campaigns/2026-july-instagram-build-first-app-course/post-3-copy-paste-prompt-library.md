## Caption (exact)

Most apps need the same handful of features: a way to search, a way to delete, a message that says "saved!", a menu to get around. So we wrote the prompts for you. 💌

Swipe through, save the post, and adjust the brackets to your own app when you need one.

Two things to notice about how they're written. Each prompt asks for one feature and describes it precisely, down to the exact words on the screen. And the last prompt asks Lovable to test what it built, including the case that should fail. That habit catches problems while they're still small and cheap.

These come with our Build Your First App with Lovable course on LittleParrot.app, along with login and voting prompts. The first challenge is free.
