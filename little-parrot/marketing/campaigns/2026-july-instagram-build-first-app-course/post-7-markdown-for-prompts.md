## Caption (exact)

The ## and ** symbols you see in well-structured Lovable prompts are markdown, a simple way to format text. It's how Lovable reads the structure of your request. 📝

Headings turn your prompt into clear sections. Numbered lists say "these steps happen in this order". Bold flags the constraints that must not be missed. Backticks say "use this exact text", which is how your button ends up saying precisely what you chose.

The lovely part: markdown works almost everywhere in the AI world. Structure a request this way in a chat assistant like ChatGPT or Claude.ai and you'll get more precise answers there too. Learn it once, use it in every tool.
