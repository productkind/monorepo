## Caption (exact)

Somewhere in its training data, your chat assistant read a million LinkedIn posts, and the loudest ones left the deepest impression. Ask it for "a LinkedIn post" and out come the rockets, the dramatic pauses, the hashtag avalanche. 🚀

The repair takes three lines. A tone block that names your actual voice ("understated, sincere, plain wording, no buzzwords"). Context about what really happened, because the model invents whatever you leave blank. And if you want the shortcut: paste a past post of yours as a sample, and it will mirror your patterns instead of the gurus'.

The second-guessing ("too formal? too braggy?") is what keeps many of us from posting at all. Handing that part to a well-briefed assistant, with your voice as the brief, lowers the cost of being visible.
