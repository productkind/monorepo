## Caption (exact)

There's a moment most of us know: a technical term keeps circling the stand-up, everyone nods, and you nod too, filing it under "look up later". Later rarely comes, and the term returns next week. 🙋

The two-minute fix is a prompt with three details: the level you need ("keep it simple, I'm a PM, no jargon"), the context ("how is this used in a product like ours?"), and the purpose ("so I can follow the discussion"). Purpose is the ingredient people skip, and it gets you an answer shaped for your meeting rather than a lecture.

Then ask follow-ups until it makes sense. An analogy, a connection to a feature you know, the questions a PM should be asking about it. Nobody is watching, and no question is too basic.
