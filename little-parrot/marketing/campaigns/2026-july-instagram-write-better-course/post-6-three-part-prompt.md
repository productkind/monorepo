## Caption (exact)

If you keep one card from this series, make it this one. Nearly every prompt that disappoints is missing one of three parts. 🧾

The request: what you want, in one line. Tone and style: how it should sound, and how long. Context and background: everything the model can't know on its own, because whatever you leave blank, it fills with plausible guesses.

A status nudge leans on the tone part. A release update leans on context. A LinkedIn post needs both, plus a sample of your writing. Same skeleton every time, which is why it's worth remembering once.

And a note for the builders here: this is the same structure our app-building courses use for prompting Lovable. Requests, tone and design, context about your users. Master it on messages, and you've already learned half of vibe coding.
