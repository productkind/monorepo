## LinkedIn (two posts, spaced)

1. **Day 0:** post `linkedin-personal.md` (Kinga). Both links (SheBuilds apply and the
   free course) go in the post body, not the first comment.
2. **Day +2:** post `linkedin-company.md` (productkind).

Spacing them keeps the personal and company posts from competing in the same feed.

Both links sit in the body: LinkedIn penalises a post when the author drops links in
the first comment, so a comment is worse than carrying two links in the post itself.
