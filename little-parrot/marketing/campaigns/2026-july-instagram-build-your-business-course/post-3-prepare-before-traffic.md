## Caption (exact)

The tempting order is: share the link everywhere today, sort out the details later. The problem is what "later" costs you. Visitors who can't leave an email are gone for good, and the ones your analytics never saw taught you nothing. 🕸️

Our course teaches the patient order: test your one-sentence value proposition on real people, set up a domain and a professional email (the only money you'll spend, under $25), add a wait-list form with a small lead magnet, and install analytics before anyone arrives, because your first visitors are the most valuable ones to learn from.

Then you share the link everywhere, and every single visitor either stays in touch or teaches you something. Usually both.
