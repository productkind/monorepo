## Caption (exact)

After the people you know, where do the first strangers come from? For most small products the answer is startup directories: free, browsable lists where early adopters go looking for new tools. 🧭

The craft is in the submission. Your one-liner is the value proposition you already tested (this is why testing it came first). Your image is a screenshot of the product doing its job, because a logo tells a browser nothing. And your paragraph names the pain, in the words your users use.

Then submit to two or three, and watch your analytics: directories tag their links, so you'll see precisely which ones send visitors. That's your signal for where to invest next, and it's a lovely feeling the first time a stranger arrives from one.
