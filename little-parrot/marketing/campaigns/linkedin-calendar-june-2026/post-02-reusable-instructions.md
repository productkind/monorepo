# Post 02 — Fri 5 Jun

**Angle:** I stopped re-explaining myself to AI.
**Transferable tip:** Capture a repeated prompt as a reusable instruction, not a one-off chat.

---

For months I explained the same things to AI over and over. How our courses should sound. What a good outline looks like. The words we never use, like "game-changing", or any em dash.

At some point I stopped retyping it and wrote it down once, in a file the AI reads before it writes anything for me.

Now when I ask for a course outline, it already knows our standards. When I draft a post, it already knows the words we avoid. I'm not starting from zero each morning.

This is a big part of why two of us can do the work we do. We don't have more hours than anyone else. We stopped spending them repeating ourselves.

I add to that file most weeks, usually right after the AI gets something wrong in a way I can finally put into words.
