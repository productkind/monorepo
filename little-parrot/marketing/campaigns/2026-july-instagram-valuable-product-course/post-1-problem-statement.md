## Caption (exact)

You know the problem you want to solve better than anyone, and that knowledge is the most valuable thing you bring to building an app. This template is how product managers put it to work. ✍️

Five blanks: who the user is, what she's trying to achieve, what's stopping her, why that barrier exists, and how it makes her feel. Written from her perspective, with your solution deliberately left out.

It takes ten minutes, and it earns its keep for months. Every time you're unsure whether to build a feature, you check it against the statement. Every prompt you write to an AI builder gets sharper because you know exactly whose problem you're solving.
