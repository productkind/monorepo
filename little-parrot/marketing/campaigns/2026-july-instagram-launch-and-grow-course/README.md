# Instagram: Launch and Grow Your Lovable App (6 carousels)

Six Instagram carousels built from the **Launch and Grow Your Lovable App**
course (`lovable-publishing-00`) and its toolkits, shaped by
`productkind/marketing/strategy/audience-research.md` and the content strategy.

## Why these six (research trace)

- **This course IS our strongest demand data.** The audience's clearest asks
  after the first build are publishing, sharing, domains, and discoverability,
  and "production ready" is their own bar for real. Every post serves the
  post-first-build moment where the research says everyone else abandons them.
- **Five of the six repackage existing toolkit items** (meta tags guide,
  legal prompt library, SEO checklist, feedback question bank, launch
  checklist), per the reuse-first Instagram rule.
- **Saveable formats win saves:** setup guides, decision tables, checklists,
  and question banks throughout.
- **The GEO post (4) is first-in-category content:** nobody in this niche
  teaches getting recommended by chat assistants, and the topic is native to
  our audience's tools.

## Posting notes

- Account: Little Parrot Instagram. Cadence 1 to 2 per week, so this set
  covers three to four weeks. Suggested order: post in numbered order; it
  mirrors the launch journey (go live → build trust → get found on Google →
  get recommended by AI → listen to users → grow steadily).
- CTAs name the full course title (Launch and Grow Your Lovable App) and
  LittleParrot.app, because the bio holds only 5 links and we have 8+
  courses, so posts can't rely on "link in bio". The UTM URL below remains
  for wherever the course link is actually placed (a bio slot, stories,
  comment replies):
  `https://littleparrot.app/ff736c4f-8ccc-41b6-96c6-68806804c5d8/course-overview?utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=launch-and-grow`
  (add `&utm_content=post-1` etc. per post for per-post tracking).
- The course's first challenge (Go Live: Your First Real Launch) is free;
  CTAs say so.
- Carousel slides: 1080 x 1350 (4:5 portrait), same visual system as the
  other two July sets so the account reads as one series.
- Sequencing with the other campaigns: this set naturally follows the
  build-first-app set (same Dalmie/Book Club Organiser storyline, next stage
  of the journey). If both run in the same period, alternate them.
