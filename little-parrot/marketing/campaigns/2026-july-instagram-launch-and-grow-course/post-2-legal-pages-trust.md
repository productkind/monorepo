## Caption (exact)

When your app's users are friends, trust is a given. The day someone you've never met finds it, they look for different signals: a privacy policy, terms of use, a way to contact a real person. 🔍

The carousel has the decision guide from our launch course: which pages you need for your situation, a free way to generate them (Termly, or a chat assistant with the right opening prompt), and the exact Lovable prompts for footer links and a cookie banner.

One honest note: these prompts get you started, and they aren't legal advice. If your app handles sensitive data or payments, a conversation with a lawyer is worth it.
