## Caption (exact)

There's a moment that stings a little: you send your app to a friend, and the link preview shows a random URL and a default icon. The app works, but it doesn't look real yet. 🙈

The fix takes about fifteen minutes, and it's five details: a favicon, a clear title, a one-line description, a share image, and a URL you can say out loud. Swipe through for where each one lives in Lovable, plus a prompt that asks Lovable to check your setup at the end.

These small touches change how people treat your app before they've tapped a single button. First impressions form in seconds, so give your app a fair one.
