“Make me an app for my book club” gets you something generic. Vague prompts get vague results. A structured prompt gets you much closer. 👉🏼

LITTLEPARROT.APP03 / 07

Start with two sections: what the app is, then what the user does, step by step.

YOUR PROMPT 
## Product overview
A simple app where book club members suggest books for the group to read next.

## User flow
1. User opens the app and sees the list
2. User adds a book with a short note
↑

Written in markdown, the simple formatting Lovable reads.
