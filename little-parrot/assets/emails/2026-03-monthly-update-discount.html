Introducing the Toolkit

Each course now comes with its own Toolkit you can use while you build: prompt templates, cheat sheets, troubleshooting guides, pre-publish checklists, and more. Two courses have their Toolkits ready, and we're gradually adding them to every course.

Your learning experience got smoother

We fixed a lot of things that were getting in your way. Several of you reported video issues, mobile quirks, and loading frustrations. We fixed every one of them.

  • Video playback works properly now Videos no longer freeze, the seeker bar works, and you can mute on iOS too. Thanks to Berto and Julia for flagging this 🙏
  • Mobile is much better The hamburger menu is back, the Nest pages fit on smaller screens, and you no longer need to tap "Previous" twice to go back.
  • No more loading spinners in courses We replaced them with subtle skeletons so you're never staring at a blank screen.
  • Smoother navigation You can browse courses without leaving your Nest, and the top bar takes up less of your screen so you can focus on learning. Thanks to Mostafa for pointing this out ✨
  • Prompts available right where you need them When we encourage you to try something in Lovable, the prompt template is now right there in the same step. No more going back to find it. Thanks to Charlotte, Simon, and Evelien for this suggestion 🤩

New features to help you learn

  • Navigate back to completed challenges Revisit any challenge you've already finished, right from the course overview. No more losing track of where that one useful tip was. Thanks to Fanni for this feedback 🤗
  • Credit estimates in the Intro to Lovable course Before each prompt, you can now see roughly how many Lovable credits it'll cost. No more surprises mid-build. Thanks to Natalia, Maria, Zsofi, Marketa, Liso, Pop, Simoni, Neha, and Maya for raising this 🌟
  • Video transcripts Prefer reading over watching? Every video now has a transcript you can open with one click.
  • Feedback button on every step See something unclear or wrong? Flag it directly and we'll fix it. Your feedback shapes the course content.

Community

We launched the Little Parrot Discord community. It's a space to ask questions, share what you're building, and connect with other women who are learning alongside you. If you haven't joined yet, look for the link in your Nest.

A fresh new look

You may have noticed: Little Parrot got a visual overhaul. New logo, new brand colours, and a bolder design language across the platform. We wanted the experience to feel as edgy and welcoming as the community we're building.
