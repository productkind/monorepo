## 🧰 A clearer Toolkit

We rethought our Toolkit categories to make them clearer. Every item now carries one of these five labels, so you know how to use it before you open it:

- **Template**: Fill in with your own details.
- **Guide**: Follow the steps to set something up.
- **Prompt library**: Paste into your AI app builder.
- **Checklist**: Work through before or during a task.
- **Cheat sheet**: Look up when you need it.

👉 [Browse the full Toolkit](https://littleparrot.app/nest/toolkit/?ref=discord&auth=login)

## 🚀 SheBuilds by Lovable is back

SheBuilds is a 48-hour buildathon. You get free Lovable credits to build your idea, alongside hundreds of other women building theirs. The top prize is one-to-one mentorship with **Elena Verna**, Head of Growth at Lovable.

Apply, especially if hackathons have felt like they're for someone else. SheBuilds was made so more women are the ones building with AI.

👉 [Apply for SheBuilds](https://shebuilds.lovable.app/?ref=littleparrot.app)
