# What we built for you in March

Hi everyone,

We're sharing our monthly update on what we've been working on. March was a big month.

## 🧰 Introducing the Toolkit

Each course now comes with its own Toolkit you can use while you build: prompt templates, cheat sheets, troubleshooting guides, pre-publish checklists, and more. Two courses have their Toolkits ready, and we're gradually adding them to every course.

👉 [Explore the Toolkit](https://littleparrot.app/nest/toolkit/?ref=discord)

## Your learning experience got smoother

We fixed a lot of things that were getting in your way. We fixed every one of them.

- **Video playback works properly now** — Videos no longer freeze, the seeker bar works, and you can mute on iOS too. Thanks @Berto and @Julia for flagging this 🙏
- **Mobile is much better** — The hamburger menu is back, the Nest pages fit on smaller screens, and you no longer need to tap "Previous" twice to go back.
- **No more loading spinners in courses** — We replaced them with subtle skeletons so you're never staring at a blank screen.
- **Smoother navigation** — You can browse courses without leaving your Nest, and the top bar takes up less of your screen so you can focus on learning.
- **Prompts available right where you need them** — When we encourage you to try something in Lovable, the prompt template is now right there in the same step. No more going back to find it. Thanks @Charlotte, @Simon, and @Evelien for this suggestion 🤩

## New features to help you learn

- **Navigate back to completed challenges** — Revisit any challenge you've already finished, right from the course overview. Thanks @Fanni for this feedback 🤗
- **Credit estimates in the Intro to Lovable course** — Before each prompt, you can now see roughly how many Lovable credits it'll cost. No more surprises mid-build. Thanks @Natalia, @Maria, @Zsofia, @Marketa, @Liso, @Pop, @Simoni, @Neha, and @Maya for raising this 🌟
- **Video transcripts** — Prefer reading over watching? Every video now has a transcript you can open with one click.
- **Feedback button on every step** — See something unclear or wrong? Flag it directly and we'll fix it. Your feedback shapes the course content.

## A fresh new look

You may have noticed: Little Parrot got a visual overhaul. New logo, new brand colours, and a bolder design language across the platform. We wanted the experience to feel as edgy and welcoming as this community.
