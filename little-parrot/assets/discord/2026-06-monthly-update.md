# What we built in June

Hey everyone,

We kept hearing the same thing from you: your Lovable credits were running out way too quickly. So this month we built a course to fix exactly that. And a few more things besides.

## 💸 Stop wasting credits on small fixes

A typo fix or an icon swap shouldn't cost you credits. Our new course, [**Save Lovable Credits: Edit Your App Like a Developer**](https://littleparrot.app/7171137b-9461-425f-8737-ad3400d56fd8/course-overview?utm_source=discord), shows you how to make those small changes yourself, on your own computer, for free.

You'll also learn to use GitHub Copilot, a free AI agent built into your code editor that makes the bigger changes for you. Your credits then go to the work that really needs them.

👉 [See the new course](https://littleparrot.app/7171137b-9461-425f-8737-ad3400d56fd8/course-overview?utm_source=discord)

## 🏆 Certificates for every course you finish

When you finish a course, you've built a practical skill. Now you can show it. You earn a **Certificate of Completion** for every course you complete, including ones you finished before today. Open a completed course and click **Your Certificate** to grab it.

There's an **Add to LinkedIn Profile** button right on it, so your network can see what you can now do.

If you post about your certificate on LinkedIn, tag us at [productkind](https://www.linkedin.com/company/productkind/) so we can cheer you on.

👉 [Open My Learning](https://littleparrot.app/nest/my-learning/?utm_source=discord&auth=login)

Lovable keeps moving things around, and a course that points at a screen that has changed leaves you stuck. So we brought two courses fully up to date, with new steps and screenshots. The bug-fixing one now opens with a smoother, less wordy first challenge too.
