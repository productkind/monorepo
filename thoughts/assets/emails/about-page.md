# Welcome to Thoughts by productkind!

### Practical, hands-on articles for people building products.

### What you can expect

I write for anyone involved in building products. Whether you're defining the vision, solving technical challenges, refining the user experience, or just getting started, my intention is to help you move forward with a bit more clarity and confidence.

Each article offers something you can use: actionable steps, a shift in mindset, or a way to navigate messy or complex situations. I don't claim to have all the answers, but I hope Thoughts by productkind helps you feel a little more at ease with the uncertainty that comes with working in tech.

I write when I have something useful to say rather than on a fixed schedule, so when an email from me arrives, it's because I believe it's worth your time.
